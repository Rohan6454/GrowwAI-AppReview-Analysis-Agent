# Phase 7: Email Draft Generation - Evaluations

## Success Metrics
- **Format Compliance:** 100% of emails include subject line and properly structured body
- **Content Inclusion:** All required elements from weekly note included
- **Professional Tone:** 95% of emails meet professional communication standards
- **Gmail Draft Creation:** Draft is created successfully in Gmail via MCP and remains editable
- **Readiness:** 100% of drafts are ready-to-send without additional editing
- **Generation Speed:** Create email draft within 15 seconds
- **Consistency:** Similar inputs produce consistent email structure

## Quality Checks
- **Subject Line Quality:** Clear, concise, and informative subject lines
- **Body Structure:** Logical flow from summary to details to recommendations
- **Grammar and Spelling:** Error-free professional writing
- **Call-to-Action Clarity:** Clear next steps or decisions requested
- **Recipient Appropriateness:** Tone suitable for intended audience (executives)

## Agent Rules
- Draft email content only from the weekly report and source insights; do not add new findings or invent follow-up items.
- Do not create or imply recipient-specific details, approvals, or attachments unless explicitly provided.
- Ensure the email draft is transparent about what is based on actual review evidence.

## Performance Benchmarks
- Handle email generation for various report lengths and complexities
- Consistent formatting across different email clients
- Memory efficient template processing
- Error handling for malformed inputs

## Testing Approach
- Email client compatibility testing (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
- Human review of tone and professionalism
- Automated format validation
- Template rendering tests
- Performance benchmarking
